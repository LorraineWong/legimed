# Legimed — Your Medication, Made Legible

> Powered by Gemma 4 · Offline · Multilingual · Apache 2.0

Turn any medication leaflet into a personalized visual guide that anyone can understand.

## The Problem
Medication leaflets are 5,000 words of medical jargon at 8pt font.  
760 million people can't read them. 2.2 billion can't see the text clearly.  
125,000 people die from preventable medication errors every year (WHO).

## The Solution
1. Upload a medication leaflet PDF
2. Answer 5 quick questions about your health profile
3. Get a personalized A4 visual guide — dosage timeline, side effects, food interactions, warnings
